Privacy policy

Design content — replace before launch

Dr.Swift Diagnostics ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect personal and health information when you use our website, app, and at-home collection services.

Information we collect

  • Account details: name, email, phone number
  • Health and lab data: test orders, results, and profiles you manage
  • Booking details: address, collection preferences
  • Usage data: app and website interactions for service improvement

How we use your information

We use your data to schedule collection, process tests in partner labs, deliver reports, provide insights, and communicate about your orders. We do not sell personal health information.

Consent for care delivery

I agree to share patient and booking details with Dr.Swift care staff, phlebotomists, and accredited partner labs to complete this test.

Data retention

Account, booking, and report data are retained while your account is active and for the period required for clinical and legal records.

Clinician review

Reports are informational and for clinician review. They are not a diagnosis or treatment plan.

Urgent care

If you have severe or urgent symptoms, seek emergency care immediately. Do not wait for lab results.

Sharing

We share data with accredited partner laboratories, phlebotomists, and technology providers as needed to deliver the service. Doctors may access reports only with your consent.

Your choices

Manage communication preferences and privacy settings on our Privacy choices page or contact support.
