Our mission

Make preventive testing easier to start and easier to understand

Health data often gets scattered across PDFs, WhatsApp forwards, and forgotten folders. Dr.Swift keeps the journey simpler: choose a panel, complete sample collection at home, and receive results with reference ranges, history, and context in one place.

The goal is not to replace a doctor. It is to help families arrive at the next appointment better prepared, with results that are readable, shareable, and easier to compare over time.
