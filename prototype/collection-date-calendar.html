  • Behavior: Calendar button opens a popover (not a full-screen modal). Selecting a day closes the popover, checks a custom day radio, and writes a readable date into the field (e.g. Thu, 24 Jul 2026).
  • Quick picks: Choosing Today / Tomorrow clears the custom chip and resets the date field to “Today” / “Tomorrow”.
  • Rules: Past dates disabled; range = today → +30 days (phlebo booking window). Easy to change later.
  • Mobile: Popover stretches under the sample row so it stays tappable.
